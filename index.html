About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and AI risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments.

Working in partnership with UK universities and industry organisations, IDR bridges academic insight with real-world implementation through training programs, applied research and operational risk projects.
