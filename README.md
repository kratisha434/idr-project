# Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) – Homepage Design

## Overview

This project presents a responsive homepage and brand concept for the **Institute of Digital Risk (IDR)** — an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and AI risk. The goal of the design is to clearly communicate IDR’s mission, training model, and community while maintaining a modern and professional visual identity suitable for a technology-focused institution.

## Logo Design

The IDR logo uses a **geometric cube-inspired form** to represent structure, multidimensional risk, and resilience in digital systems. The cube metaphor reflects how modern digital risk spans multiple layers including cyber security, AI governance, and operational technology risk.

The colour palette uses **orange, black, and white**:

* **Orange** represents innovation, energy, and proactive risk management.
* **Black and white** provide a clean and professional technology aesthetic.

The logo is designed to remain **legible at small sizes**, allowing it to function effectively in mobile navigation bars and favicon contexts.

## Website Features

The homepage is implemented as a **single-page responsive website** using semantic HTML5, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript.

Key features include:

* **Responsive Layout** using CSS Grid and Flexbox
* **Sticky Navigation Bar** with smooth scrolling
* **Mobile Navigation Menu (Hamburger Menu)**
* **Hero Section** with clear value proposition and call-to-action buttons
* **Service Pillars Section** highlighting IDR’s core model:

  * Academy
  * Innovation & Incubation
  * Advisory Services
* **Pipeline Model** visualising the Train → Hire → Innovate → Deploy framework
* **Community Section** describing IDR’s audience and sectors served
* **Contact / Register Interest Form**

## Design Approach

The design emphasises:

* **Clarity and readability**
* **Minimalist technology aesthetic**
* **Strong hierarchy of information**
* **Accessible colour contrast**

Subtle animations and hover effects are used to enhance user experience while maintaining a professional tone suitable for an academic–industry institute.

## Design Decisions

Several design decisions were made to ensure the homepage communicates IDR’s mission clearly and professionally:

**1. Cube-inspired visual motif**
The logo and hero background incorporate a geometric cube concept to represent structured risk, multidimensional digital systems, and resilience.

**2. Orange accent colour**
Orange was chosen as the primary accent colour to represent innovation, urgency, and proactive risk management. It is used strategically for call-to-action buttons and pipeline elements to guide user attention.

**3. Minimalist layout**
A clean layout with generous spacing and clear hierarchy ensures readability and aligns with the aesthetic of modern technology and research institutions.

**4. Train → Hire → Innovate → Deploy pipeline**
This model visually communicates IDR’s value proposition: developing talent, enabling industry collaboration, and deploying risk expertise into real-world environments.
